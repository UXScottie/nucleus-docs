## Colours

Colour plays an important role in defining the visual identity. Using the full suite of colours in accordance with brand guidelines can create a real sense of brand recognition.

### System

System colours support and enhance communication with users throughout their journeys. Please be aware that system colours always carry specific meanings, so exercise caution to avoid confusion by using these colours or similar brand colours inappropriately. Never rely solely on system colours for conveying information or requesting actions.

## Typography

The British Gas font is a typeface unique to British Gas. It has a clear, legible and elegant style. A fallback to sans-serif is present to allow the operating system to provide the substitute.
